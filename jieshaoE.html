Kindergarten introduction
Dalian bilingual kindergarten is founded by Harvard University, alumni association of Kellogg School of Northwestern University and Montessori education experts from the United States. As a well-known bilingual pre-school education institution, the school incorporates the most advanced teaching and research achievements at home and abroad by adhering to the education philosophy most suitable for the growth of children and introducing the essence of Montessori education which has been verified for centuries ,to create The most suitable for the healthy growth of children's campus environment. After ten years of accumulation and precipitation, it has now become the flagship of leading the international development of preschool education in China.
