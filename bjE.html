ASSIGN CLASS >>
The distribution of student classes is extremely sophisticated and complex. The school allocates classes based on teacher suggestions, student interviews, and children's age, gender, and cultural background, as comprehensive considerations benefit all children. The school will be in accordance with the principle of the most conducive to the overall development of students to allocate classes to students. Parents who have special requirements for assigning classes to schools may apply in writing and submit them to the Admissions Officer on campus. Unless exceptional circumstances, once the class has been assigned, the school will not make other adjustments.
